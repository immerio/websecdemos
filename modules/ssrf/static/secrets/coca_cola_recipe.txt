---------------------------
- Citric Acid: 3 oz

- Sugar: 30 lbs (unspecified amount of water)
- Water: 2.5 gallons
- Lime Juice: 2 pints

Flavor Base (7X):
-----------------
- Alcohol: 8 oz
- Orange Oil: 20 drops
